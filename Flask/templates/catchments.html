Catchments:

Below are the catchments (and their measure) for which predictions have been produced. All predictions cover the same period which is January 2017.

It is very important to note that catchments 2, 3 and 4 contained a distinct lack of training data due to missing values. This means that the prediction results have suffered accordingly. Catchments 1 and 5 were trained with a significantly higher amount of training instances. These catchments should therefore be used to draw a conclusion about the neural networks performance for this problem.

Also note that the period of nowcasting usually only extends up to the period of 6 hours but, due to good results (and for interest), I have created predictions 9 and 12 hours out for catchments 1 and 5.
